UBVoting

April Park, Desean Abraham, Mike Alicea, Bob Shannon

The Problem

65% of people in the age range of 18-25 do not vote. This generation also happens to be the largest generation that has spawned in this United States ever. With the amount of people that are eligible to vote that do not we would have a serious impact on the popular vote.

The Solution

UBVoting is a web app that displays information on current presidential candidates. Information is drawn in from different sources like news articles, Reddit, Twitter, YouTube, and Facebook. The primary function of UBVoting is to encourage college students to stay informed and vote and be a one stop shop to gather various info on candidates quickly.
